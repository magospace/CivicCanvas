# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Texas Data Canvas a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Be mindful that civic-data work can affect real communities.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Attempts to bypass the project's data-governance or safety boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the process in `SECURITY.md` or through the repository maintainers once the public remote is configured.
